What defines an insect?





Insects can fly!


Wings have helped insects move around. So when food becomes hard to find in one area, they can fly to another place with food.
Their bodies


Insects have three pairs of jointed legs, segmented bodies, an exoskeleton, one pair of antennae, and (usually) one or two pairs of wings.
They can adapt


There are many ways that insects have changed their behavior in some way to live or feed on different plants or animals.

Their eat strange things


Insects will eat many things that we would expect them to, like plants in our gardens. But some will feed on things such as wood and some feed on one plant.
